Release of Avengers : Infinity War


Theatrical

Avengers: Infinity War held its world premiere at the Dolby Theatre in Los Angeles on April 23, 2018, screening also at the adjacent El Capitan Theatre and Grauman's Chinese Theatre.[159] It was released in most countries worldwide, including the United States, on April 27, 2018, with a few debuts beginning as early as April 25,[116] and was shown in IMAX and 3D on select screens.[160][161] In the United States, the film opened in 4,474 theaters, 408 of which were IMAX; this was the widest release for a Disney title ever and the second-widest ever after Despicable Me 3's 4,529 theaters. Three of AMC Theatres' locations screened the film for 24 hours straight, with 53 of their locations having showtimes at either 2 am or 3 am to accommodate demand.[162] In India, the film had the biggest release ever for a Hollywood film, opening on nearly 2,000 screens in four languages.[163] The film also screened in 515 4DX theaters in 59 countries.[164] Avengers: Infinity War was originally scheduled to be released on May 4, 2018 in the United States.[93][94] The Chinese release of the film, which opened on May 11, 2018,[165] was originally scheduled to end on June 10, 2018, but was granted a "rare" extension of 30 days, to end on July 9, 2018.[166] Avengers: Infinity War is part of Phase Three of the MCU.[167]
